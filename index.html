Understanding Phishing Threats

What is Phishing?

The issue of website phishing is a major security concern in the digital era. Phishing involves deceptive attempts to obtain sensitive information by disguising as a trustworthy entity in digital communication.

Why Does This Project Matter?

A Forbes report noted that in 2022, the United States faced over 500 million phishing attacks, affecting more than 300,000 people and resulting in financial losses exceeding $52 million.

The Risk in Everyday Transactions

Online activities like shopping on platforms like Amazon and using credit cards daily can expose users to vulnerabilities, especially the elderly who may struggle with verifying the authenticity of websites.

The Goal

To combat phishing, this project aims to create a machine learning model trained to recognize key characteristics of authentic websites, thus identifying sites as phishing, suspicious, or legitimate.
